# Move Like You
*Philip Rehberger*

---

[Intro - Philip talking]
Yeah... Highlands Ranch, baby
This one's for Liam
Seven years old, watching everything I do
Including the way I move...

---

[Verse 1]
We were in the kitchen, dinner cleaned up
Music on the speaker, I was sippin' from my cup
Started bobbing my head to a Brooks and Dunn track
Liam climbed up on a chair, mirrored everything I had

I leaned to the left, he leaned right back
I spun on my heel, he tried the same move and laughed
Couldn't quite stick the landing, but he caught the beat
Seven years old, big grin, two left feet

---

[Chorus]
He said, "Daddy, I've been watchin' you
I'm your sidekick man, I wanna move like you
Down in the kitchen with mama 'fore the lights come down
Spin around twice and stomp on the ground
I do everything you do
So I've been watchin' you"

---

[Verse 2]
Last weekend was a wedding, friends out east of town
Reception in a barn, twinkle lights all around
I grabbed Maria's hand, took her out to the floor
Two-stepped through a slow song, then a couple more

Looked over my shoulder and there's Liam in his tie
Hand on Ava's shoulder, leading her — eye to eye
Same two-step I just taught Maria one song back
Seven years old, dance card already stacked

---

[Chorus]
He said, "Daddy, I've been watchin' you
I'm your sidekick man, I wanna move like you
Out on the dance floor when the lights come down
Take her by the hand and spin her around
I do everything you do
So I've been watchin' you"

---

[Bridge]
And it ain't always pretty — sometimes I lose my ground
Sometimes I cuss at traffic with the four of 'em around
But the boy don't see the slipups, he just sees the way I move
And every step I take, he's gonna take it too

---

[Verse 3]
Came home from work last Tuesday, walked in through the door
Liam had Maria spinning slow across the kitchen floor
Same song I'd put on the night before for her and me
Seven years old, leading mama, just like he'd seen me

I leaned against the doorway, watched the whole thing through
Maria caught my eye and mouthed "he learned it all from you"
And right then I knew I better keep my steps in line
'Cause every move I make, he's making it in time

---

[Final Chorus]
He said, "Daddy, I've been watchin' you
I'm your sidekick man, I wanna move like you
In the kitchen with mama 'fore the lights come down
Spin around twice and stomp on the ground
I do everything you do
So I've been watchin' you"
