"Clap, Tessa, clap" — Ava clapping with her
Tessa watching Ava, copying her sister
Two little hands smacking off-beat in a row
Ava saying "good job, Tessa" — Tessa got the glow

"Iyla, your turn" — Iyla pull peekaboo
Hide them little hands, three years old, on cue
Pull 'em away — Tessa cackling like she don't care
Whole rug rolling laughing, can't a one of us spare
