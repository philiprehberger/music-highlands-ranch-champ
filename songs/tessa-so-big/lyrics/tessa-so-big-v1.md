[Verse 1]
What it do, Tessa baby in the highchair locked in
Daddy pulled up close, here's where it begin
"Wave" — Tessa waving, opening and closing them fingers
Little baby wave, I tell you it lingers

"Clap" — Tessa clapping, both hands smacking the air
Off-beat little smacks but she don't even care
She clapping, she grinning, she watching me close
Highchair tray hopping like a little disco

"Peekaboo" — hide my face behind my hands
Pull 'em away — Tessa losing it, she fans
Belly laugh from the highchair like she invented it
One year old, comedy committed
